4 things to consider when pairing fonts:

  1. Are both fonts legible?
  2. Consider pairing two fonts of different types, like a serif and a cursive.
  3. Pay attention to your specific case - sometimes you only need one kind of font and sometimes you will need three or four.
  4. Play around a bit! Don't just settle with the first two fonts you find.
